# Marketing Track: Build Community and Awareness

Spread the word about MassGen and grow the community.

---

## What Marketing Contributors Do

- Create content for social media
- Write blog posts and tutorials
- Build partnerships with other projects
- Engage with the community
- Recruit new contributors

---

## Who Should Join This Track

- Content creators and writers
- Social media managers
- Community builders
- Partnership development professionals
- Anyone passionate about sharing MassGen
