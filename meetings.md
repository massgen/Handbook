# Meetings

MassGen holds two types of regular meetings to coordinate contributions.

---

## Research Meetings

**When:** 6:00pm PST, [Schedule TBD]

**Purpose:**
- Identify and refine long and short term goals
- Propose concrete case studies to achieve short term goals
- Generate features that need to be developed

**Who Should Attend:**
- Research track contributors
- Anyone with feature proposals
- Team members interested in roadmap planning

**Agenda:**

1. **Identify and refine long/short term goals**
   - Discuss project vision and roadmap
   - Prioritize objectives
   - Review progress on goals

2. **Propose case studies around short term goals**
   - Define what should be achieved concretely
   - Specify measurable outcomes
   - Plan implementation approach

3. **Propose features based on case studies**
   - Connect features to case study outcomes
   - Discuss technical approach
   - Estimate effort and timeline

**After Meeting:**

Create GitHub issues for:
- Case studies discussed
- Features proposed
- Any bugs identified

---

## Daily Sync Meetings

**When:** 5:30pm PST, every day

**Purpose:**
- Share progress updates from each track owner
- Coordinate across all tracks (research, dev, docs, marketing)
- Identify and address blockers
- Align on priorities for the next day

**Who Should Attend:**
- Track owners (required)
- Active contributors (encouraged)
- Anyone with updates or blockers

**Meeting Format:**

Quick round-robin updates (2-3 minutes per person):
- What I worked on today
- What I am working on tomorrow
- Any blockers?

**How to Join:**

1. Join Discord: [discord.gg/VVrT2rQaz5](https://discord.gg/VVrT2rQaz5)
2. Request meeting link in #massgen channel
3. Attend your first meeting as an observer

---

## Meeting Best Practices

**Prepare Your Update:**
- Have your update ready before the meeting
- Keep it concise and focused (2-3 minutes)
- Note any blockers ahead of time

**During Meetings:**
- Stay on topic
- Respect time limits
- Take notes on action items
- Ask questions if unclear

**After Meetings:**
- Follow up on action items
- Update GitHub issues as needed
- Share meeting notes if you took any
