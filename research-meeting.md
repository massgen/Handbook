## Meeting Format

### Explore Research Ideas

Present well-motivated research problems and discuss intuitive solutions.

Contact Yu (crinvo) on Discord if you want to present. All are welcome!

**Guidelines:**
- The idea can be directly connected to MassGen or to broader multi-agent systems
- There is no strict time limit

**Template:**

Please prepare a short PPT or Word document with the following sections:
- **Problem:** The problem you are trying to solve (or the limitations of existing work/system)
- **Motivation:** Why the problem is important to solve
- **Solution:** An intuitive solution (a high-level philosophy of how the solution addresses the problem)

### MassGen Improvements

Discuss goals, case studies, and features for MassGen development.



**Template:**

Please prepare a brief document or notes covering:
- **Problem:** A concrete problem you want to solve
- **Case Study:** A concrete example with specific prompts
- **Current Output:** What MassGen produces now with these prompts
- **Expected Output:** What MassGen should produce after improvements
- **Features:** Specific features needed to bridge the gap
